# EmailShieldPro

EmailShield Pro is a free Windows desktop application that protects your inbox from phishing attacks, scams, and malicious emails.

EmailShield Pro connects to your Outlook and Gmail accounts and automatically scans your emails for suspicious links, spoofed senders, and phishing patterns, giving each email a clear risk rating so you know exactly what's safe to open.

## Features

- Automatic phishing and threat detection for Outlook and Gmail
- Risk ratings for every email — High, Medium, and Low
- Automatic background scanning
- Full scan history to review past results
- Safe List to whitelist trusted senders
- Quarantine for high risk emails
- Customisable notifications for high and medium risk emails
- Multi-account support for Outlook and Gmail
- Secure local storage — your data never leaves your device
- Windows DPAPI encryption for stored credentials
- Dark mode support
- 100% free — no subscriptions, no ads, no hidden costs

## Download

Download EmailShield Pro for free from the [Microsoft Store](https://apps.microsoft.com/store/detail/emailshieldpro).

## Requirements

- Windows 10 version 1803 or later
- x64 processor
- Internet connection for email scanning

## Privacy

EmailShield Pro stores all data locally on your device. No data is ever transmitted to external servers.
