EmailShield Pro — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 March 2026

Thank you for using EmailShield Pro. This Privacy Policy explains how EmailShield Pro handles your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Who We Are

EmailShield Pro is developed and maintained by Harry Bolitho. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at support@emailshieldpro.com.

2. Data We Collect

EmailShield Pro does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data handled by the app remains entirely on your local device.

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • OAuth authentication tokens for your Outlook and Gmail accounts
  • Email scan history and risk ratings
  • Safe list entries (trusted senders)
  • App settings and preferences

3. How We Use Your Data

All data stored by EmailShield Pro is used solely to provide the app's core functionality:

  • Authentication tokens are used to connect to your email accounts via Microsoft Graph API and Google Gmail API
  • Scan history is stored so you can review past results
  • Safe list entries are used to exclude trusted senders from threat detection
  • App settings are stored to preserve your preferences between sessions

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally in your Windows user profile under %LocalAppData%\EmailShield_Pro\. All stored data is encrypted using Windows Data Protection API (DPAPI) with user-scoped encryption, meaning only your Windows user account can access it.

5. Third Party Services

EmailShield Pro connects to the following third party services solely to scan your emails:

  • Microsoft Graph API — to access Outlook emails. Subject to Microsoft's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gmail API — to access Gmail emails. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

No data from your emails is transmitted to any servers operated by EmailShield Pro.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any of your data with third parties. Ever.

7. Children's Privacy

EmailShield Pro is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@emailshieldpro.com.
